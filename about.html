About Me

My story started in Mexico City, later fatefully transplanted to the East Village neighborhood of Chicago, where I lived most of my life—only living in Manhattan, NY, for a web design role where I learned all about advertising and marketing.

Now, husband, father of three, and a self-proclaimed technophile living in Austin, Texas, I spend most of my free time building furniture and cowbells, iOS native apps, and make-believe I play the ukulele.

I am pursuing a Front-end Web Development degree, which I look to complete in March of 2024.

Education

At age 17, fresh out of high school, I decided to study abroad, return to my birthplace, and study Architecture at one of the top Mexican universities in México City.

Once in the U.S., I continued my incredible learning experience via a more creative and less technical practice. I decided on Graphic design given my previous experience and fascination with graffiti and its culture. I attended Columbia College in the South Loop area of Chicago.

After College and in the middle of an economic recession, I participated in a UX training program. Given my education and experience, I was an ideal candidate and ultimately transitioned to User Experience.

Skills

At the heart of my UX approach is a deep-rooted understanding of users gleaned from various research methodologies and frameworks. This understanding informs the intuitive structure I craft through Information Architecture and Design.

As I move to wireframes and prototyping, every interaction is designed to be fluid and user-centric, validated through continuous Usability Testing. My commitment to Visual Design ensures aesthetic appeal, while cross-platform design guarantees device consistency. Every design decision prioritizes Accessibility and inclusivity, ensuring no user is left behind.

Throughout this process, collaboration and clear communication with stakeholders and teams ensure that our designs align with user needs and business objectives.

In sum, my holistic approach weaves these skills together, delivering user experiences that are both delightful and functional. Trust me to take your user experience to the next level.
